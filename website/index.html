Presence awareness

Your owl is awake when you're working, drowsy when you're idle, and asleep when you're away. Friends see your state at a glance.

Friends on your desktop

When a friend comes online, their owl appears right on your screen. It's like glancing across a shared office.

👋

Wave and chat

Send a quick wave to say hi, or chat through cute speech bubbles. Low-pressure, high-warmth communication.

Jump on a call

When a wave turns into a conversation, hop into FaceTime with one click. No links, no scheduling, just "hey, got a sec?"
